About Happy Donuts

We are a team of dreamers


We are creative people who want to do something original. Our main specialisation is production donuts. We have 20 different species donuts but for commercial goals we production only 10 species donuts. We experiment all the time, trying to come up with new recipes for everyone.

Also we have another specification, we can decorate donuts according to your own ideas. Design can be easy or hard, for our team it isn't difficult. Because all of our employees learned from the best pastry chefs. And, of course, we really enjoy watching how people's reactions.
